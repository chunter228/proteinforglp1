How to Prevent Muscle Loss on GLP‑1: Expert Tips

To preserve muscle while on GLP‑1 therapy, focus on three pillars: resistance training, adequate protein and nutrient timing. Experts recommend at least two days of strength training per week to stimulate muscle synthesis. Pair workouts with 20‑30 grams of protein soon after to maximize recovery.
