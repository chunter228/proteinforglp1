Protein‑Packed Meal Plans for GLP‑1 Users

Putting theory into practice, here’s how a day of eating might look for someone on GLP‑1 therapy. Breakfast could be a veggie omelet or tofu scramble with whole‑grain toast. Lunch might feature grilled salmon over quinoa and roasted vegetables. A mid‑afternoon snack could be Greek yogurt or a Koia shake, and dinner could be a lentil curry with brown rice.

Aim for 20‑30 grams of protein per meal and 10‑15 grams per snack. Remember to include fiber‑rich carbs and healthy fats to aid satiety and vitamin absorption.

If you struggle with a reduced appetite, smoothies and soups can deliver nutrients in smaller volumes. Listen to your body and adjust portion sizes accordingly.
