What to Eat After Your Injection: Snack Ideas

Some GLP‑1 users experience nausea or reduced appetite after injections. Choosing gentle, protein‑rich snacks can help you meet your nutritional needs without discomfort. Options include a slice of whole‑grain toast with nut butter, a small bowl of oats with berries, or a creamy plant‑based shake.

Experts advise against relying on sugar‑laden snacks or skipping meals entirely. Protein combined with complex carbohydrates stabilizes blood sugar and aids recovery.
