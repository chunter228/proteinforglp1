About Us

GLP1 Protein Authority exists to help people using GLP‑1 medications like Ozempic and Wegovy stay healthy, strong and informed. GLP‑1 drugs reduce appetite and promote weight loss, but they can also lead to muscle loss and nutrient deficiencies. Our goal is to provide nutrition guidance, meal ideas and expert insights so you can maintain muscle mass and overall wellbeing while on treatment.

What Experts Say

Patients taking GLP‑1 drugs often lose fat‑free mass and need regular exercise and adequate protein to preserve muscle. — UVA Health researchers [1]
People who stop taking GLP‑1 drugs tend to regain weight quickly because the medication stops working once discontinued. — Dr. Louis Aronne [1]
GLP‑1 medications have been linked to increased testosterone levels in men with obesity. — Endocrine Society study [1]
Protein helps preserve muscle and supports immunity, hormone production and metabolism. — Registered Dietitians [1]
